I am an Engineer working in the field of Robotics, with a keen interst in Computer Vision, Physics and everything Nueral Networks. Currenty developing softwares for Addverb Technologies, one of the hottest startups in the ecosystem with a strong impact globally. My daily routine involves tinkering with a lot of underdevelopment (pun-intended) robots mainly Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs) inorder to develop and test new methods to solve existing problems mostly related to localisation, mapping and perception, and, sometimes (a lot many timers though) everything else robotics, beginning from interfacing and fixing lower level drivers to networking problems faced by a fleet of such systems. Building a vision for the future of robots and a team to realise the same excites me and thereby interviewing talented individuals who are passionate for Technology, forms a great part of my work which I really value.
